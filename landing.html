The basic idea of Bangalore with regards to different factors.

Bangalore

Banglore is situated in south easten Karnataka so it is very close to both the borders of Andhra Pradesh as well as Tamilnadu. The name Bangalore comes from an ancient name called Bendakaluru meaning a town but Bangalore is called Bengaluru itself in the 9th century itself which is found by the babru inscription. And this inscription is really wonderful which also includes data about a battle fought in bengaluru in the old times, the inscription also presented the various dresses at that time and their festivals. Karnataka is especially unique in the world itself for its diverse and amazing scale of inscriptions.

Physical Features

Bangalore gets almost 970ml of annual rainfall, which is one of the highest for major cities in india. This rainfall is possible due to a phenomenon called the palakkad gap. Bangalore is situated in Dharwar craton which is one of the oldest. The most important feature of bangalore is the high rainfall and its altitude.
